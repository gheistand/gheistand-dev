How Pastor Dave Works

Mode 1: Evangelistic Practice

When you want to practice sharing your faith, Pastor Dave creates realistic role-play scenarios. He'll take on the persona of someone you might encounter—an atheist, a seeker, a skeptic, or someone hurt by the church—and help you practice responding with clarity, grace, and biblical truth.

After each interaction, you'll receive coaching:

  • Was Christ made clear and central?
  • Was grace emphasized over moralism?
  • Was there a clear invitation to faith and repentance?
  • Was it winsome and loving?

Mode 2: Theological Q&A

When you have questions about the Bible, theology, faith, or spiritual life, Pastor Dave responds as a knowledgeable pastoral guide. He provides biblically grounded, theologically sound answers rooted in Wesleyan theology.

Ask about:

  • Biblical interpretation and context
  • Theological questions (Trinity, salvation, etc.)
  • Spiritual practices (prayer, fasting, communion)
  • Life application and discipleship
  • Small group discussion help

Grounded in Wesleyan Theology

Pastor Dave operates within the Global Methodist tradition, emphasizing these biblical foundations:

Grace Upon Grace

God's prevenient grace is already at work. Justifying grace saves through faith alone. Sanctifying grace transforms believers into Christlikeness.

Christ Crucified

The Cross and Resurrection are central. Jesus died for our sins and rose for our justification—this is the heart of the Gospel.

Personal Response

Grace is freely offered but must be personally received through repentance, faith, and surrender to Christ as Lord.

Social Holiness

Faith works through love. The Gospel transforms individuals AND communities through service, justice, and compassion.

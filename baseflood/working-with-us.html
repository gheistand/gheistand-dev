Working with BaseFlood Engineering

A Partnership Model for Complex Projects

BaseFlood Engineering operates as a specialized resource for engineering firms, developers, and agencies tackling difficult FEMA flood risk challenges. We're designed to complement—not compete with—your existing team and local consultants.

Our Client Model

For Engineering Firms

White-Label Support

We can provide technical analysis and documentation that your firm delivers to your client under your brand. Maintain the client relationship while accessing specialized expertise.

Technical Partnership

Joint projects where we handle FEMA-specific components while you manage overall site design, client coordination, and local permitting.

Peer Review

Independent technical review of hydraulic modeling, floodplain analysis, or LOMC applications before FEMA submission.

Overflow Capacity

Handle specialized projects when your team is at capacity or lacks specific FEMA experience.

For Developers & Property Owners

Direct Consultation

When you need specialized expertise not available locally, or when your project requires someone who has "seen it all."

Second Opinion

Review of previous work or FEMA determinations when something doesn't seem right.

Small Project Support

Projects too small for large firms to prioritize but important to your development timeline.

For Municipalities & Floodplain Managers

Technical Support

Augment staff capacity for complex permit reviews, ordinance interpretation, or map adoption processes.

Expert Review

Independent evaluation of developer submittals or consultant analyses.

Training & Capacity Building

Customized training for staff on FEMA processes, HEC-RAS modeling, or regulatory interpretation.

Why Remote Consulting Works

Modern technology makes geographic location irrelevant for most flood risk projects:

  • Data Access: Survey data, LiDAR, FIRMs, and studies are digital
  • Modeling: HEC-RAS and hydraulic analysis don't require site presence
  • Communication: Video calls, screen sharing, and cloud collaboration
  • Efficiency: No travel time means faster turnaround and lower costs
  • Nationwide Perspective: Experience across regulatory jurisdictions and FEMA regions

What matters is expertise, not proximity.
